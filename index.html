In Dammam, with Scholars!

Alhamdulillah, I had the privilege of visiting Prince Mohammad Bin Fahd University (PMU), a leading university in Saudi Arabia. It was a fruitful meeting with the Dean of the Research Development, Directors of the Learning Resource Center (LRC) and the Professional Development Center (PDC) at PMU. As discussed, they have agreed to strengthen mutual collaboration and support between PMU and UIU. I also had the opportunity to meet esteemed academics and scholars representing Saudi Arabia, Australia, Brazil, Canada, Sudan, India, Pakistan, and Bangladesh. Special thanks to Prof. Dr. Md. Aminul Islam, Research Professor at PMU, for arranging my visit to the university.
